ZINES + EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS

SO, WHAT ARE THEY?

Our zines are illustrated workbooks and essays about audio electronics, salvage, and building musical instruments from trash. They are meant to be read, folded, scribbled in, and left on your bench until the pages curl.

The textbook series walks through hands-on projects; the free press and smaller prints round out the library with essays, diagrams, and oddities.

SHOP THE ZINES

The Disengineering Textbook Series

Three volumes span illustrated project walkthroughs, deeper circuit ideas, and the occasional rant. Each book is risograph-printed, staple-bound, and designed to survive coffee rings and solder flux.

IT’S GOT FOLD OUTS

Large schematics and assembly drawings pull out from the center so you can keep the instructions open next to your build without squinting at tiny type.

WHO IS IT FOR?

Educators, workshop hosts, noise musicians, art students, and anyone who learns best with a highlighter and a pile of scrap parts. No degree required — curiosity and a willingness to read a datasheet help.

VOLUME 1

The first volume introduces core soldering, signal flow, and several complete instrument builds from salvaged consumer electronics.

CHAPTERS:

  • Bench setup & safety
  • Reading schematics
  • First oscillator
  • Contact mics & pickups
  • Simple amplifiers

VOLUME 2

Volume two goes deeper into filters, distortion, and control voltage — still grounded in projects you can finish on a kitchen table.

CHAPTERS:

  • Op-amps in audio
  • Distortion stages
  • Envelope followers
  • Sequencers & logic
  • Case studies from workshops

VOLUME 3

Wasted Writings

Essays, diagrams, and stray thoughts that did not fit neatly into a build chapter — still useful, still cranky about planned obsolescence.

TABLE OF CONTENTS:

  • Right-to-repair notes
  • Salvage taxonomy
  • Interview fragments
  • Collage & layout studies
  • Reading list

Disengineering Free Press

The Free Press is our occasional broadsheet: news from the collective, reprints from workshops, and contributions from friends. Grab a copy when we table at events, or ask us to slip one into your order.
