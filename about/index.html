ABOUT

The Disengineering Society is a hardware hacking collective and a publisher of educational materials. Our textbooks reimagine educational literature through their uniquely playful construction and workshop-specific applications. These publications go hand-in-hand with our widely offered hardware-hacking workshops that emphasize chaos, destructive instruction, electronic screeches, and a devout commitment to combating “planned obsolescence” in modern technology.
